- Hobbies -

Well, firstly, coding is one of my favorite hobbies. I've been scripting on a platform called ROBLOX for about 3-4 years now, and it's become a known passion of mine to my friends. I taught myself how to program on it, and I imagine it's what pushed me to be so invested in CS.

I was fascinated by the games on ROBLOX, and I wanted to make my own. But I soon realized that in order to do so, I would need to know how to program in ROBLOX's version of the Lua language. So, I began researching on my own different syntax, skills, questions, errors, and in general things I wanted to know how to do. In the process, I also taught myself how to animate, use 3D modeling softwares/applications like Blender, and create graphic art.
