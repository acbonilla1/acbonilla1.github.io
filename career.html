At the moment, I can't be too sure of what I want to pursue as a profession later in life. But right now what interests me is programming and stem.


Game Designer

"Game designers work with a team to design and develop video and computer games. Learn about the training, skills, salary and employment outlook for this occupation to see if this is the right one for you." - Study.com

Median Annual Salary: $105k

One should know how to work in a team. This designer should know how to program in different languages, work with computer systems, and just in general manage a project. You need a Bachelor's degree in most cases.



Computer Programmer

What a computer programmer does can vary, but generally they work with code in order to organize information, design/create webpages, and just in general express ideas online in hand with the Internet.

Median Salary: $84k

Although the level of education required by the job varies between workplaces, generally it would be helpful to have a certificate or assosciate's degree as well as a good amount of experience in the field. A bachelor's or master's degree would help, too, but with a lack thereof, one could make it up with experience.
