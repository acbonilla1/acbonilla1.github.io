Andrew Bonilla's Portfolio

Farlands is my current main project on ROBLOX at the moment.

Farlands is a free-roam adventure game that I started making in the December of 2019.

Although I've taken a lot of pauses in the development of it, the time I have spent on Farlands is definetely shown in the gameplay and code.
I love working on this project because it fuels a passion that allows me to continue learning and enhancing my skills not only as a programmer, but a model/interface designer, animator, graphics artist, and more.

The most important thing for me in a game is my ability to have fun, so that's exactly what I sought out for when I started this game.

I don't want to profit much off of this project- I mostly just mess around with code and play with my friends.
This game has evolved a lot over the years, and I'm excited to see where it goes!

A good example of my improvement over the years can be shown through the change in logo design for the game.

As I improved my programming skills, I also grew in subjects like graphic/art design.
